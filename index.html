About Us

Founded in 2015, Texas Web Design began with a simple mission: to provide Texas businesses with world-class websites at affordable prices. What started as a small team of passionate developers has grown into the state's premier web design agency, serving hundreds of satisfied clients across diverse industries.

Our core values of innovation, integrity, and client success drive everything we do. We believe in creating not just beautiful websites, but powerful business tools that generate real results. Our team of expert designers, developers, and digital strategists work tirelessly to ensure every project exceeds expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your hosting package?

Our enterprise-grade hosting includes SSL certificates, daily backups, 24/7 monitoring, DDoS protection, and CDN integration. We guarantee 99.9% uptime and provide unlimited bandwidth.

How long does it take to complete a website?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Complex e-commerce sites may take 4-6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages that include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Our team is available 24/7 for emergency assistance.
